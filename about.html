About us

Welcome to Baco, a minimalistic wine e-shop founded by four wine enthusiasts who also happen to be students at IT University Copenhagen. We're passionate about wine and believe that it's more than just a drink - it's an experience.

Our small shop is located in the heart of Copenhagen, and we take pride in offering a curated selection of wines that we've personally tasted and handpicked. Whether you're a seasoned wine connoisseur or just starting to explore the world of wine, we've got something for you.

At Baco, we believe that good wine should be accessible to everyone, which is why we strive to keep our prices affordable without compromising on quality. We named our brand after the Celtic god of wine, symbolizing our commitment to providing exceptional wine experiences.

Thank you for choosing Baco, and we look forward to sharing our love of wine with you.
